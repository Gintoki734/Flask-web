You can say and write the date in Spanish everyday in class. This is a great way to practise and develop your Spanish skills!

## Days

| Spanish    | English    |
|------------|------------|
| lunes      | Monday     |
| martes     | Tuesday    |
| miércoles  | Wednesday  |
| jueves     | Thursday   |
| viernes    | Friday     |
| sábado     | Saturday   |
| domingo    | Sunday     |

*(Tip: The days of the week in Spanish all start with a small letter instead of a capital like in English.)*

### Saying what day it is
To say 'Today it is…' you can use the phrase:

**Hoy es el…** + the day

Examples:

- *Hoy es el jueves* – Today it is Thursday
- *Hoy es el viernes* – Today it is Friday

![Image](/static/4b.png)

### Asking what day it is
To ask 'What day is it today?' you can say:

**¿Qué día es hoy?**

## Dates

### Giving the date today
To say 'Today is…', you can use the phrase:

**Hoy es** + day + number + month

Examples:

- *Hoy es martes el cinco de marzo* – Today it is Tuesday the 5th of March
- *Hoy es sábado el once de junio* – Today it is Saturday the 11th of June

### Asking the date today
To ask 'What is the date today?' you can say:

**¿Cuál es la fecha hoy?**

(*la fecha* means 'the date' in Spanish.)

![Image](/static/7c.png)

### Did you know?

To say 'the 1st', use **el primero**. This is the only **ordinal number** you use in dates.

Example:

- *Hoy es miércoles el primero de octubre* – Today is Wednesday the 1st of October

For all other dates, use **cardinal numbers** (e.g., two, three, four, five).

Example:

- *Hoy es lunes el dos de diciembre* – Today is Monday the 2nd of December

## Birthdays

### Saying your birthday
To say 'My birthday is…', use the phrase:

**Mi cumpleaños es el…** + number + month

Examples:

- *Mi cumpleaños es el dos de mayo* – My birthday is the second of May
- *Mi cumpleaños es el doce de diciembre* – My birthday is the 12th of December

![Image](/static/2b.png)

### Asking someone's birthday
To ask someone 'When is your birthday?' you can say:

**¿Cuándo es tu cumpleaños?**

(*cuándo* means 'when.')
